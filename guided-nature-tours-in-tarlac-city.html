Frequently Asked Questions About Guided Nature Tours

How much do the guided nature tours at Aces Private Resort in Tarlac cost, and what’s included in the price?
Our guided nature tours start at ₱1,500 per person for a half-day experience and ₱2,500 per person for a full-day tour. The price includes the services of a licensed local guide, bottled water, access to exclusive nature trails within and around Tarlac City, and all necessary safety equipment. We also provide complimentary snacks for full-day tours. Group discounts are available for parties of 8 or more—please contact us for a personalized quote.
Do I need to book my guided nature tour in advance, and how far ahead should I reserve?
We highly recommend booking your guided nature tour at least one week in advance, especially during weekends and holidays when demand is high in Tarlac City. This helps us secure your preferred date, ensure guide availability, and arrange any special requests. Last-minute bookings may be possible depending on availability, but advance reservation guarantees the best experience.
Are your guides certified and is Aces Private Resort licensed to operate tours in Tarlac?
Yes, all our nature guides are fully certified by the Department of Tourism and have extensive experience leading tours in the Tarlac area. Aces Private Resort is a duly registered and licensed tourism establishment, compliant with all local and national regulations governing eco-tourism and nature tours in Tarlac City.
What is the process for joining a guided tour—where do we meet and how long does the tour last?
On the day of your tour, please check in at the Aces Private Resort front desk at least 30 minutes before your scheduled departure. Our guide will meet your group in the lobby, provide a short orientation, and distribute any needed gear. Half-day tours typically last 3-4 hours, while full-day tours run about 7-8 hours including breaks. Transportation to the trailhead is provided if the route starts off-site.
What happens if it rains or if the tour needs to be cancelled? Is there any guarantee or refund policy?
Your safety is our top priority. If weather conditions are unsafe or if a tour must be cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer a reschedule or a full refund. For guest-initiated cancellations, we provide a 100% refund if you cancel at least 48 hours before your scheduled tour. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a fee, but we always strive to accommodate our guests and find a suitable solution.
